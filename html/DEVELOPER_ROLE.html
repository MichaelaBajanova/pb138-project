DEVELOPER ROLE

Summary

Designs and/ or codes components to meet solution specifications.

Mission

Ensures building and implementing of ICT applications. Contributes to low-level design. Writes code to ensure optimum efficiency and functionality and user experience.

  • Shape documentation
  • Provide advanced, component technical support
  • Resolve issues prior to and following testing
