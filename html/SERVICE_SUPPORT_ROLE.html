SERVICE SUPPORT ROLE

Summary

Provides remote or onsite diagnosis or guidance to internal or external clients with technical issues.

Mission

Provides user support and troubleshoots ICT problems and issues. The primary objective is to enable users to maximize their productivity through efficient and secure use of ICT equipment or software applications.

Deliverables

Accountable

    Responsible

    • First Level Support
    • Incidents Database

    Contributor

    • Solved Incident
    • Escalation Process
    • Second Level Support

    Tasks

    • Identify and diagnose issues and problems
    • Categorize and record reported queries and provide solutions
    • Support problem identification
    • Advise users on appropriate course of action
    • Monitor issues from start to resolution
    • Escalate, if needed, unresolved problems to a higher level of support
    • Provide essential online security advice and support
